Investor Protection

Six layers
protecting us.

Before you put in a dollar, you should know exactly what stands between your capital and any downside. Here's every layer.

01 Escrow Account Your money sits in a dedicated escrow account from day one. We can only access it for verified, documented project expenses. Nothing moves without a paper trail.
02 PPM Documentation Before you commit, you receive the full Private Placement Memorandum. Every term, every right, every obligation. In writing. Filed under SEC Regulation D.
03 Real Estate Attorney A licensed real estate attorney is on every transaction. Not a formality. They review every contract, every closing, every document before anything is signed.
04 Quarterly Reports Every 90 days you get a plain-language update. Where the project stands, what's been spent, what's coming next. No jargon, no spin.
05 Hard Asset Backing Your investment is backed by a real property. Land and structure in Park City, Utah. Not a promise. A physical asset with real market value.
06 Accredited Investors Only We only work with accredited investors under SEC Regulation D. This keeps the deal structured, the process clean, and your legal standing protected.

Have questions about how your capital is protected? We'll walk you through every layer before you commit to anything.
